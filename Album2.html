Album Appraisal

A raw, unfiltered debut rooted in Tottenham reality and early-nineties independence Rizzio establishes his persona through humour, confrontation and coded mythology The Sally narrative introduces the named-woman system while street philosophy drives the core themes Production is skeletal but purposeful, reinforcing the DIY ethos rather than limiting it Tracks like Tottenham and Nasty Iz Nicety define the moral and environmental tension of the record Moments of absurdity and repetition act as deliberate disruption rather than filler The album closes by looping its own language, signalling the beginning of a wider canon A foundational text that prioritises identity, doctrine and voice over polish
