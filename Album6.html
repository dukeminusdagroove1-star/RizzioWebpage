Album Appraisal

EQUALOPPORTUNITYNASTIES (MAR 1996) is the expansion phase where Rizzio weaponises contradiction into doctrine. The record oscillates between satire, street reportage, and raw provocation, binding Project X into a unified but unstable force. Tracks like “Renegades” and “Injustice” anchor the socio-political spine, while “Nikki’s Nikkers” and “Cautious My Precious” expose the unchecked libido driving the persona. The Lon-Town mythos fully crystallises here, mapping movement, tension, and hierarchy across the city. Crucially, the “Here To A Special No Thank Heaven’s” arc mutates—Part Six fractured, Part Seven ascendant—signalling transition from chaos toward authored myth. This is not refinement; it is controlled sprawl, where excess becomes identity and narrative density becomes power.
